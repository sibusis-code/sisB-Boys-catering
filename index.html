Our Story

Baked with Love,
Served with Pride

SisB & Boys Catering & Event was built on a simple belief: every celebration deserves food that delights from the very first bite.

Based in Olievenhoutbosch, we specialise in creating custom cakes, cupcakes, and full catering experiences for all kinds of events. Whether you want a towering tiered cake, delicate cupcakes, or a full spread for your event, we bring skill, creativity, and heart to everything we bake.

Every item is made to order — because you deserve something uniquely yours.

Boniswa Mdunana
Managing Director & Head Baker
